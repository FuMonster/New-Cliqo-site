Trust and Security Information

Our data protection measures include:

We commit to the following Service Level Agreements:

  • 24/7 customer support.
  • Response times for support tickets based on severity levels.

Security Practices

Our security practices include:

  • Employee training on security awareness.
  • Strict access controls and authentication mechanisms.
  • Regular security audits and compliance checks.

Privacy Policies

We are committed to protecting user privacy. Our privacy policies include:

  • Transparency in data collection and usage.
