Security, Compliance, and SLA Information

Security

Our platform employs industry-standard security measures, including encrypted data storage, secure code practices, and regular security audits to ensure the safety of our clients' data.

  • Data Encryption: All sensitive data is encrypted both at rest and in transit using AES-256 encryption.
  • Access Controls: We implement strict access controls to ensure that only authorized personnel have access to sensitive information.
  • Incident Response: Our incident response plan includes immediate action to mitigate any potential security breach.

Compliance

We adhere to several industry standards and regulations, ensuring compliance in all aspects of our service delivery.

  • GDPR Compliance: We are committed to protecting the privacy of our European clients and comply with the GDPR regulations.
  • ISO 27001 Certification: We have achieved ISO 27001 certification, demonstrating our commitment to information security management.
  • HIPAA Compliance: For clients in the healthcare industry, we comply with HIPAA regulations to safeguard patient information.

Service Level Agreement (SLA)

Our Service Level Agreement outlines our commitment to providing high availability and reliable service.

  • Uptime Guarantee: We guarantee 99.9% uptime for our services, ensuring that our clients can rely on our platform.
  • Response Time: Our support team is committed to responding to all inquiries within one business day.
  • Compensation: In the unlikely event of non-compliance with our SLA, we offer compensation in the form of service credits.
